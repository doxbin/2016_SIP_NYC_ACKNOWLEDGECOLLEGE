Major Problems at College Campuses

As you begin to look into colleges or if you are already in college, it's important to be aware of the problems that occur in college and to see if it's safe or not.

College Campus Problems:

Drug and Alcohol Abuse
- In some colleges high levels of drug use and heavy drinking can be seen. This is a problem that should be recongnized by all college studets and those getting ready for college.
Violence
- On many campuses there are high leves of violence. There are murder cases,shootouts and other types of violence. It's important that all students are aware of this issue and know that this can occur in a college.
Sexual Assaults
- On some college campuses there may be sexual assaults and it's a major problem that students should know
Gender & Sexual Discrimination
- Since many colleges are very diverse, there has been an increase in gender & sexual discrimination in which students question others sexual identitites and some aren't accepted beacuse of thier gender.
Hazing
- Hazing involves harassment, abuse or humiliation used as a way of initiating a person into a group and in college, many students are involved in groups or clubs like sports teams, sororities, and other organizations. So this is another major problem students should be aware of.
